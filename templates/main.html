Chose the more convenient class for you
First Class

First-class accommodations are decorated luxurious. You will have your own wardrobe rooms, private baths. All first-class accommodations are equipped with telephones, heaters.

In addition, you will have priority access to the gymnasium, swimming pool, turkish baths, dinning salon, etc.

Second Class

Rooms are composed of 2 to 4 berths built into the walls of the cabins. To have a better privacy a curtain around to bed is available. Each second-class cabin had a washbasin and a chamber pot but the bathrooms are common.

Third Class

Third-class rooms have 4 to 6 bunk beds.

Like second-class passengers, they share bathrooms, but the number of people sharing a bathroom is higher. Only two bathtubs are available for all 710 third-class passengers, one for the men and one for the women.
